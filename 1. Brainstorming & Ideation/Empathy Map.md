# Empathy Map

## User: Local Resident
- **Thinks & Feels:** Anxious during heavy rains. Needs assurance and timely information.
- **Hears:** News about potential floods, warnings from neighbors.
- **Sees:** Rising water levels in nearby rivers/drains.
- **Says & Does:** Prepares emergency kits, seeks information online.
- **Pains:** Lack of accurate, localized warnings.
- **Gains:** Peace of mind, time to evacuate or protect property.
